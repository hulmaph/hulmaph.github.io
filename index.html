About Hulma

Hulma has the best Marikina shoemakers has to offer - high quality and customizable stylish footwear!

 

Hulma was founded by Janina Halagueña with a goal of bridging the local shoemakers from Marikina City with different customers to increase and sustain their artisanal jobs. Handmade in Marikina, Hulma features boots, loafers, shoes, and sandals in designs that maintain the high-quality aspect they are best known for as well as stylish characteristic that makes it worth flaunting.

The Marikina Shoe Industry

The shoe industry of Marikina can be traced down to the story of a man's trailblazing spirit that started because of a very ordinary problem - repair the shoes he has that was purchased aborad. This man did his best to repair the shoes, which then led to the promulgation of the industry throughout.

The industry is still very much alive, but is however facing a crisis - the arrival of foreign producers has brought tough competition to the local shoemakers of Marikina. However, what still stays true throughout everything is what makes their produce distinct from competitors - their value for high-quality footwear.
